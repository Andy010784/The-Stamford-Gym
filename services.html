Services


Here at The Stamford Gym we offer a wide range of services


  • Our clients have access to our in-house personal trainers, we have both male and female trainers available (fees apply)
  • Why not book an appointment with our very own onsite dietian, who will give you some expert nutritional advice
  • Yoga classes are held every Monday, Wednesday and Friday from 1.30 p.m. and 7.00 p.m. (fees apply)
  • We have an onsite cafe serving delicious healthy food and drink
  • You can also enjoy our accessible shower and changing facilities, with complimentry tolietries and towels

Equipment


We have a full range of equipment in the gym, including:


  • A large stretching mat for pre workout, warm up and warm down.
  • Exercise bikes and treadmills
  • Cross trainers and stair climbers
  • Rowing Machines and Stepper Machines
  • Dumbbells of various weights, Kettlebells and stretching bands
  • Bench presses, Squat presses and pull up bars
  • Medicene balls, punch bags and speed balls
  • Cable machines, leg presses and smith machines
