Gym equipment


We have a full range of equipment in the gym, including:


Stretching mat:   Pre workout warm up or warm down, stretching bands are icluded in this area.

Treadmills:   Helping you improve your cardiovascular fittness, you can walk, jog, run, and even set it up on an incline. Strengthening your leg muscles. Quads, hamstrings and calves, whilst also improving your stability by engaging your core muscles. A fantastic way to burn calories!

Rowing Machines:   Giving you a complete cardiovascular workout, targeting your upper back muscles but also working your legs, arms, shoulders and your core all at the same time.

Stair Steppers:   A difficult but rewarding cardiovascular workout, targeting your lower body muscles. Quads, hamstrings and calves whilst Strengthening your glutes and hip flexors.

Cross Trainers:    give you a complete full body cardiovascular workout. Using your upper and lower body muscles at the same time. Mainly focusing on your leg muscles. Quads, hamstrings, and calves but also your upper body muscles. arms, shoulders and back.

Free weights:    We have a variety of dumbbells, kettlebells, barbells. with a dedicated area with a number of weight benches provided. Lifting free weights allows you to work multiple ranges of motion and muscles in one exercise. helping you to stabilize your movement and build strength, power, and coordination.

Stationary Bikes:   Stationary bikes are a fantastic piece of exercise equipment for targeting a variety of muscle groups. targeted muscles when on astationary bike include the muscles in the legs, glutes, and core. The pedaling motion of a stationary bike primarily works the quadriceps, hamstrings, gluteus maximus, and calves.

Smith Machines:   These machines are for all-round strength training. equipped a barbell fixed to a guided vertical track. Giving you controlled strength exercises. squats, lunges, bench presses and shoulder presses, whilst giving you the security and stability. A range of muscle groups are targeted with a variety of ways to set the machine up.

Chest Press Machines:   Targeting your chest muscles. Allowing you to push weight away from your body, adjusting your hand position helps you focus on different areas of your chest, upper or lower sections.

Bicep Curl Machines:   Targeting your bicep muscles, allowing you to perform curls with added resistance.

Tricep Extention Machines:   Targeting your triceps muscles by extending your arms, allowing you to work on Strengthening the triceps.

Leg Press Machines:    Helping you Strengthen your quads by pushing weights away from your body.
