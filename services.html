Our Services

Here at The Stamford Gym we offer a wide range of services

Our clients have access to our in-house personal trainers, we have both male and female trainers available (fees apply) Why not book an appointment with our very own onsite dietian, who will give you some expert nutritional advice Yoga classes are held every Monday, Wednesday and Friday from 1.30 p.m. and 7.00 p.m. (fees apply) We have an onsite cafe serving delicious healthy food and drink You can also enjoy our accessible shower and changing facilities, with complimentry tolietries and towels
